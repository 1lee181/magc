Operation Project a16z.2

Project Name: Operation Project a16z.2

Client: McMaster Venture Capital Club (MVCC) - Open to all faculties. Slogan: Learn • Invest • Build

Target Users: McMaster students, primarily mobile users (60%+ of web traffic from Statista). QR codes are placed around campus including club events, posters, and club fairs for easy access.

Static Information: Club name, open membership policy, contact email, event links, general member signup, Club SubStack, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

Key Design Feature: Live stock market tracker of the S&P 500 at the top of the webpage.

Data & Content: Past events, executive team members, MVCC partners/startups, club history, and embedded general member signup form.

Interactive Elements: Active animations and seamless web design with continuous scrolling and intuitive exploration.
